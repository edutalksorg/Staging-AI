## Handling Common Questions

### Q: "How accurate is the AI pronunciation analysis?"
**Answer**:
> "Great question. Our AI achieves 92-95% accuracy compared to human expert evaluations. We use phonetic analysis algorithms similar to those in Duolingo and Rosetta Stone. The AI analyzes phonemes, stress patterns, and intonation. While it's not perfect, it's consistent and provides instant feedback, which is crucial for rapid improvement."

---

### Q: "What about data privacy?"
**Answer**:
> "Data privacy is critical. We're GDPR compliant and follow industry best practices:
> - All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
> - We don't sell user data to third parties
> - Users can delete their accounts and all associated data anytime
> - Audio recordings are stored securely and can be deleted by users
> - We're working towards ISO 27001 certification"

### Q: "How do you handle cheating in quizzes?"
**Answer**:

> - For institutional use, we can enable proctoring features like webcam monitoring and tab-switching detection"

## Body Language Guide

### Confident Posture
- Stand straight, shoulders back
- Feet shoulder-width apart
- Weight evenly distributed
- Avoid swaying or pacing

### Hand Gestures
- Use open palms when explaining
- Point to screen when referencing features
- Count on fingers when listing items
- Avoid crossing arms or hands in pockets

### Eye Contact
- Scan the entire room
- Hold eye contact 3-5 seconds per person
- Don't stare at slides or notes
- Engage with questioners directly

### Voice Modulation
- Vary pitch and tone
- Emphasize key words
- Pause for effect
- Speak louder for important points
- Slow down for technical details
